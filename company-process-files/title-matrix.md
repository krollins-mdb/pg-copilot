## Notes

- This is not a checklist, nor is it a complete list of the criteria for promotions and performance evaluations. This is a guide for you to understand management's general expectations and should serve as a starting point for discussion with your manager regarding your performance and role.

- The expectations at each level are cumulative. All expectations from previous levels apply to subsequent levels.

- Writers will move into new roles based on their performance and business needs; there is no set time period (minimum or maximum) that an engineer will spend in any one role.

- If you have any recommended adjustments to this doc, reach out to Jon DeStefano
