This document provides examples for how you can incorporate your ERG impact in your P\&G reflection. Showcase ERG impact in P\&G by sharing: What you delivered and how that created business impact, how you lived our values, and what **[Leadership Principles](https://help-hr.mongodb.com/hc/en-us/articles/16714170962707-Leadership-Principles-)** you demonstrated.

This should be a part of an ongoing P\&G conversation with your manager on your ERG work - proactively sharing your goals ([**ERG Strategic Pillars**](https://docs.google.com/presentation/d/1Q6VV4NAr63zHhmqW9mxZu5ScETVJ8s4WL9v62YX5jss/edit#slide=id.g1491a1f77d6_0_2349)), your core work & ERG responsibilities, impact you've made, and values you demonstrated. This is an opportunity to continuously align with your manager on your ERG impact and how it ties into your overall career development and leadership growth. In other words, your manager should not read about your ERG work for the first time in your performance self-reflection.

**Career Progression Framework:**

ERG work and impact closely aligns with the Leadership Commitment dimension as all employees are required to demonstrate our Core Values and Leadership Principles. Depending on your level, there are increased expectations to be a role model of our values and leadership principles, helping others to grow and develop. A detailed zendesk page can be found ([here](https://help-hr.mongodb.com/hc/en-us/articles/27914140319251-Career-Progression-Framework)).
